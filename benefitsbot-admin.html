Field Reference Guide

Use the numbers below to match each setting to its field on the left.

  1. 1
    Logo
    Upload your agency or personal brand logo (PNG, JPG, SVG). Appears in the widget header and admin sidebar.
  2. 2
    Primary Color
    Main brand color used for the widget header, buttons, and agent name. Default: black.
  3. 3
    Accent Color
    Secondary color for highlights and call-to-action buttons. Default: white.
  4. 4
    Agency Name
    Your agency or business name. Shown in the widget header and chat intro message.
  5. 5
    Agent Name
    Your first name or display name. Used in the chat greeting and the confirmation screen after signing.
  6. 6
    Agent Phone
    Your direct phone number. Displayed to prospects on the "You're All Set" confirmation screen so they can reach you.
  7. 7
    Agent Email
    Your email address. Displayed to prospects on the confirmation screen alongside your phone number.
  8. 8
    Tagline
    A short description shown under your agency name in the widget header (e.g., "Group Benefits & Insurance Advisor").
  9. 9
    Calendly Link
    Your Calendly scheduling URL. Prospects use this to book their virtual consultation at the end of the chat flow.
  10. 10
    Benefits Bot Widget Link
    After you've edited all fields to your preference and saved all settings, this is the link you copy and paste into emails and texts to send your Benefits Bot Widget to prospects. Hit "Copy Link" to grab it instantly.
